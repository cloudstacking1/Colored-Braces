What is Colored Braces?

Colored braces are traditional metal braces with customizable elastic bands that come in various colors. These bands help hold the archwire in place while allowing patients to personalize their braces with fun, vibrant shades. Popular among kids, teens, and even adults, colored braces make orthodontic treatment more stylish and enjoyable.
